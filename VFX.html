Virus Battle Visual Effects

Proposed Visual Effects for VYRUS Game

1. Infection Trails
When a virus expands to a new cell, show a glowing trail from the source to the target cell. Use a gradient effect that starts with the source player's color and fades to transparent.
2. Combat Impacts
When viruses battle, show an explosion effect at the contested cell. Use particle effects that spray in the direction of the stronger virus. Add a brief flash effect when a cell changes ownership.
3. Parameter-Specific Effects
  • Aggression: Red energy bursts radiating from aggressive virus cells
  • Defense: Blue shield-like animations around defensive cells
  • Speed: Yellow motion trails behind fast-moving virus cells
  • Stealth: Pulsing transparency effects for stealthy viruses
  • Resistance: Green barrier effects when resisting attacks
  • Virulence: Toxic green cloud effects around virulent cells
4. Mutation Effects
When viruses mutate, show a brief color shift or swirling animation. Add a small DNA helix icon that appears briefly over mutating cells.
5. Territory Borders
Highlight the borders between different player territories with a subtle glow. Make the borders pulse gently to show active contested areas.
6. Victory Animations
When a player wins, have their color gradually wash over the entire grid. Add a celebration effect at the winning player's starting position.
7. Environmental Adaptation
Show subtle background changes when viruses adapt to different grid areas. Add small environmental effects like temperature indicators.
8. Cell Growth Animation
When cells grow stronger, show a pulsing animation from within the cell. Add a small growth indicator that appears periodically.
9. Latency Effects
For viruses with high latency, show a slow, pulsing glow. Add a timer-like animation for cells in latency mode.
10. Resource Flow
Show small energy particles flowing between allied cells. Visualize resource sharing between nearby virus cells.
